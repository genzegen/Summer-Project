Help Center

Find answers to common questions and learn how to use BYR3D effectively.

Getting Started

How do I create a new room layout?

To create a new room, go to the dashboard and click on "New Project". Enter your room dimensions and start designing.

What browsers are supported?

BYR3D works best on Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. Safari may have limited support.

Using the Editor

How do I add objects to my room?

Click on the "Add Object" button, choose a 3D model from the library, and place it in the room.

How can I adjust the lighting?

Go to the "Lighting" panel and modify intensity, color, and position to enhance the realism of your scene.

Troubleshooting

Why is my project not saving?

Ensure you are logged in and have a stable internet connection. If the issue persists, contact support.

The 3D models are not loading. What should I do?

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue continues, check for browser updates.
