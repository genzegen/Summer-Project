How to use our platform?
Here are some easy steps to follow
Start Your Canvas
  • Pick your dimensions
  • Hit create and get your room
  • Start adding elements to your design
Drop and Drag
  • Click and drag to move elements
  • Place your furniture wherever you want inside the room
  • Release to place the element on the canvas
Shape Your Space
  • Place elements by dropping on desired location
  • Rotate the models by using arrow keys
  • Use the tools to fine-tune your shapes
Visualize and Save
  • Preview your design
  • Make any final adjustments
  • Click "Save" to store your design
